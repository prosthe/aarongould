About PROSA

Progressive Students Alliance - Fighting for student rights and social justice since 2015

Who We Are

PROSA (Progressive Students Alliance) is a student-based ideological and political organization at the University of Liberia, founded in 2015 by dedicated student activists.

Our Name & Meaning

PROSA derives from the combination of two Kru words:

  • Avaret: Meaning "to search for"
  • Garde: Meaning "Knowledge of Light"

Together, we are Avaret-Garde - constantly searching for the light of knowledge and enlightenment.

Our Journey

2015

Foundation

PROSA was founded by student activists at University of Liberia to address educational inequality and student rights issues.

2016

First Major Campaign

Successfully led the "Fund Our Future" campaign, securing improved student funding and resources.

2018

National Expansion

Expanded to multiple universities across Liberia, establishing a national coordinating committee.

2020

Digital Advocacy

Launched online campaigns and social media presence to reach more students nationwide.

2024

Present Day

Active in 15+ universities with thousands of members, continuing the fight for student rights and progressive values.

Our Core Ideology

Progressivism

Advocating for social reform, justice, and the advancement of education for all people, especially the common masses.

Social Justice

Fighting for equality, fair distribution of resources, and the rights of the poor and marginalized communities.

Pan-Africanism

Promoting unity and solidarity among African people and nations while celebrating our cultural heritage.

Critical Thinking

Encouraging students to analyze societal issues critically rather than accepting the status quo without question.
