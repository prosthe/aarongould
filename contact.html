Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join PROSA as a student?

You can join by filling out our membership form on the Get Involved page, attending our general meetings, or contacting any PROSA representative on your campus. Membership is open to all students who support our progressive values.

Do I need prior activism experience to join?

No prior experience is necessary! We provide training, mentorship, and support for all our members. What matters most is your commitment to our cause and willingness to learn and contribute.

How can I start a PROSA chapter at my university?

Contact our main office with details about your university and student interest. We'll guide you through the process of establishing an official chapter and connect you with resources and support.

What types of activities does PROSA organize?

We organize protests, educational workshops, community service projects, policy advocacy campaigns, intellectual debates, and social events. See our Programs page for detailed information.

How are donations to PROSA used?

Donations support campaign materials, event organization, student support programs, transportation for protests, educational resources, and operational costs. We maintain transparency in all financial matters.

Can alumni or non-students support PROSA?

Yes! Alumni can join as associate members, and non-students can support through donations, partnerships, or volunteering expertise. Contact us to discuss how you can contribute.
